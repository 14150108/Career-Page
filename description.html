Job Description

Electrical Engineers design, test, and maintain electrical systems and components. Responsibilities vary by specialization but commonly include circuit design, PCB layout review, firmware for embedded systems, system modeling, and testing.

  • Daily tasks: Design schematics, simulate circuits, prototype PCBs, debug hardware/firmware, and collaborate with cross-functional teams.
  • Tools: SPICE simulators, PCB design tools (EAGLE, Altium), MATLAB/Simulink, oscilloscopes, logic analyzers, and soldering/assembly tools for prototyping.
  • Soft skills: Problem solving, communication, documentation, and teamwork.
