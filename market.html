Job Market & Earning Potential

Electrical Engineers work across industries: power generation and distribution, consumer electronics, aerospace, automotive (EVs), telecommunications, and industrial automation. Demand is steady for engineers with hands-on design, embedded systems, and power electronics experience.

  • Hiring tips: Internships, practical projects (PCB/firmware), and experience with industry tools (SPICE, MATLAB, CAD) increase employability.
